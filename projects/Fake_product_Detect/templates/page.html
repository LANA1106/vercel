Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about detecting fake products

How accurate is your fake product detection?

Our system currently achieves 98% accuracy based on validation against known authentic and counterfeit products. Accuracy varies slightly by product category, with electronics and luxury goods being the most accurately detected. We continuously improve our algorithms as new data becomes available.

What types of products can I verify?

We support verification for over 50 product categories including electronics, fashion, cosmetics, pharmaceuticals, collectibles, and luxury goods. Our database is strongest in high-value, high-counterfeit-risk categories, but we're continually expanding to cover more everyday products.

Do you require access to my personal shopping data?

No. ScamShield never accesses your personal shopping data unless you explicitly upload or input product information for verification. We only analyze the product information you provide and do not track your browsing or shopping behavior. Your privacy is our top concern.

Can I report counterfeit sellers?

Yes! After completing an analysis that identifies a counterfeit seller, you can report them directly to relevant consumer protection authorities through our platform. We also maintain a private database of confirmed counterfeit operations to help warn other consumers.
